# HomeMade - Product Guide

## Vision
HomeMade is a collaborative kitchen management app designed to help **Eco-conscious Families** track their home ingredients, reduce waste, and discover new recipes based on what they already have. The goal is to maximize **Kitchen Efficiency** and minimize domestic food waste through intuitive management and AI-powered inspiration.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Families looking to reduce food waste and save money.
- **Secondary:** Amateur cooks seeking creative inspiration from their current pantry stock.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Seamless Management:** Effortless tracking of ingredients across shared household devices.
- **Smart Discovery:** AI-driven recipe suggestions tailored to real-time inventory.
- **Efficiency First:** A localized experience (Hebrew/RTL) that fits naturally into the daily routine.

## Key Features (Current & Planned)
- **Ingredient Management:** Add, edit, and categorize pantry items with unit support.
- **AI Recipe Engine:** Robust suggestion system using Gemini models with error handling, fallback mechanisms, real-time process tracking, and Hebrew localization.
- **Smart Shopping Lists (Upcoming):** Generate lists for items missing from suggested recipes to complete meals.
- **Secure Access:** Supabase-backed authentication for shared household data.

## User Experience & Aesthetic
- **Visual Style:** **Clean & Minimal** layout utilizing modern typography (Geist) and generous whitespace.
- **Localization:** Deeply rooted in Hebrew with native RTL support and localized culinary terminology.
- **Navigation:** Mobile-first design focusing on quick transitions between inventory and recipes.
